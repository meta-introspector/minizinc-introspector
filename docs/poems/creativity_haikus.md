## Creativity Haikus

### Creativity's Spark
Idea takes its flight,
From mind's eye, a new design,
Code, now comes alive.

New solutions, bravely forged,
Future, now unfolds.

### Discovery's Joy
Hidden truths revealed,
Patterns found, a sudden light,
Knowledge, now expands.
